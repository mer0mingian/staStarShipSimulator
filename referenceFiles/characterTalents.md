# Character Talents Reference

## General Talents

### Back-Up Plans
**Requirement:** Control 9+  
**Effect:** When you or ally fails task (if present), add 1 Momentum to group pool

### Bold (X)
**Effect:** Choose department. When buying d20s with Threat, re-roll one d20. Repeatable (different departments). Cannot have Cautious for same department

### Calm and Logical
**Requirement:** Reason 11+  
**Effect:** When gaining mood/emotional trait, may suppress it for one task/combat turn by suffering 1 Stress

### Cautious (X)
**Effect:** Choose department. When buying d20s with Momentum, re-roll one d20. Repeatable (different departments). Cannot have Bold for same department

### Close-Knit Crew
**Effect:** Scene start: if Momentum < characters with this talent, add 1 Momentum to pool

### Collaboration (X)
**Effect:** Choose department. Ally attempting task with that department: spend 1 Momentum (Immediate) to let them use your rating + one relevant focus. Repeatable (different departments)

### Constant Presence
**Requirement:** Supporting character, 2+ values, 2+ other talents  
**Effect:** Introducing this character costs no Crew Support. Gains 1 Determination first use per adventure

### Constantly Watching
**Effect:** 
- GM must spend +2 Threat for NPC to take first turn
- Re-roll 1d20 locating hidden enemies/dangers

### Dauntless
**Effect:** When intimidated/threatened, suffer 2 Stress to ignore attempt

### Extra Effort
**Requirement:** Fitness 9+  
**Effect:** Reduce task Difficulty by 1 (min 0). After completion, take Stress = original Difficulty

### Gut Feeling
**Requirement:** Insight 11+  
**Effect:** GM spends +2 Threat for reinforcements or Reversal

### Indefatigable
**Requirement:** Fitness 11+  
**Effect:** When retrying failed task in same scene, Difficulty -1

### Methodical Planning
**Requirement:** Reason 9+  
**Effect:** Ally benefiting from your plan/strategy trait: you may Assist even if not present (no turn cost in combat)

### No Hesitation
**Requirement:** Daring 9+  
**Effect:** Round start in action scene: add 1 Threat to take first turn

### No Pain, No Gain
**Requirement:** Daring 11+  
**Effect:** When failing Daring task (not opposed), may always Succeed at Cost

### Personal Effects
**Requirement:** Main character  
**Effect:** Possess significant uncommon item/device (not standard issue). Repeatable (different items)

### Quick Survey
**Requirement:** Insight 9+  
**Effect:** Scene start: ask one Obtain Information question (sensory info only)

### Reassuring
**Requirement:** Presence 9+  
**Effect:** Success with Presence: spend 1 Momentum (Repeatable) for ally to recover 1 Stress. Max 3 Stress per use

### Second Wind
**Effect:** Spend Determination at turn start to remove Defeated, recover half max Stress

### Self-Reliant
**Requirement:** Control 11+  
**Effect:** Success without buying extra dice: generate bonus Momentum = task Difficulty (cannot save)

### Studious
**Effect:** When spending Momentum on Obtain Information, ask one additional question (total, not per Momentum)

### Technical Expertise
**Effect:** Task assisted by ship's Computers/Sensors: re-roll one d20 OR let ship re-roll its d20

### Tough
**Effect:** Maximum Stress +2

### Voice of Authority
**Requirement:** Presence 11+  
**Effect:** When Assisting with Presence, add 2 Threat to treat assistance die as 1 (instead of rolling)

### Well-Informed
**Effect:** Scene start: add 1 Threat to ask GM two questions about situation/location (from contacts/rumors)

## Augment & Cybernetic Talents

### Augmented Ability
**Requirement:** Augment trait  
**Effect:** Choose attribute. Extraordinary Attribute 1 (one automatic success), complication range +2. Repeatable (different attributes)

### Durability
**Requirement:** Augment or Cyborg trait  
**Effect:** Protection 2

### Neural Interface
**Requirement:** Cyborg trait  
**Effect:** 
- Link/break with computer: minor action
- Connected: re-roll one d20 on computer tasks
- If computer/ship damaged: suffer Deadly 4 Injury with Piercing

### Sensory Replacement
**Requirement:** Cyborg trait  
**Effect:** 
- Artificial Sense trait
- Detecting hidden/concealed: re-roll single d20

## Esoteric Talents

### Empathy
**Effect:** Sense emotions of nearby beings, communicate with empaths/telepaths. Cannot turn off (except telepathy-resistant). May require task in crowds/stress

### Extrasensory Perception
**Effect:** Perceive beyond normal senses. Each hint adds 1 Threat. GM may offer info (can refuse)

### Psychokinesis
**Effect:** 
- Manipulate Close range objects mentally (personal force equivalent)
- +1 Momentum (Immediate, Repeatable): additional person's force
- Medium range: 1 Momentum; Long: 2 Momentum
- Violent force: add 1 Threat, Control + Security Difficulty 2, Stun/Deadly Severity 3

### Telepathic Projection
**Requirement:** Telepathy  
**Effect:** 
- Send thoughts to non-telepaths, hear responses
- Offensive: Presence + Security Difficulty 2 (+1 per range beyond Close), Stun/Deadly Severity 3, Piercing

### Telepathy
**Effect:** Sense surface thoughts/emotions nearby, communicate with empaths/telepaths. Cannot turn off (except telepathy-resistant). May require task in crowds/stress
