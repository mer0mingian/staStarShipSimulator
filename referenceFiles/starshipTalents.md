# Starship Talents Reference

## Defensive Talents

### Ablative Armor
**Requirement:** 2368 onwards  
**Effect:** Resistance +2

### Adaptive Shield Modulator
**Requirement:** 24th century or later  
**Effect:** 
- Modulate Shields action: Resistance +1 (additional)
- Ship may have shields raised during silent running

### Advanced Shields
**Effect:** Maximum shield capacity +5

### Backup EPS Conduits
**Effect:** When shaken and losing Reserve Power, roll 1d20. If ≤ Structure, regain Reserve Power

### Improved Hull Integrity
**Effect:** Resistance +1

### Improved Shield Recharge
**Requirement:** Security 3+  
**Effect:** Regenerate Shields action restores shields = Engineering rating +1, plus 3 per Momentum spent (Repeatable) instead of normal amount

### Point Defense System
**Requirement:** Security 3+  
**Effect:** 
- Ship has Cover against torpedo attacks (Difficulty +1)
- Does not function at warp or with Weapons system breach

### Reduced Sensor Silhouette
**Effect:** Tasks to scan/detect vessel: Difficulty +1

### Redundant Systems (X)
**Effect:** Select one system. When that system suffers breach, may activate to immediately patch it. Once per adventure

### Siphoning Shields
**Effect:** When hit by energy weapon (after resolution), may add 2 Threat to restore Shields = Security department rating

## Offensive Talents

### Adaptable Energy Weapons
**Requirement:** 24th century or later  
**Effect:** Select one energy weapon, choose alternate energy type. May use alternate type but complication range +1. Repeatable (different weapon each time)

### Automated Defenses
**Requirement:** 23rd century or later, Security 3+  
**Effect:** At end of round, if no attacks made, ship makes energy weapon attack using Weapons + Security (no assistance) against Close range target

### Expanded Munitions
**Effect:** Add one weapon (energy or torpedo). Repeatable (different weapon each time)

### Fast Targeting Systems
**Requirement:** Security 3+  
**Effect:** Targeting Solution minor action grants both benefits: re-roll d20 AND choose system hit

### High-Intensity Energy Weapons
**Requirement:** Weapons 10+  
**Effect:** If Reserve Power rerouted to Weapons, next energy weapon attack: Damage +2

### High-Power Tractor Beam
**Effect:** Tractor beam Strength +2

### Rapid-Fire Torpedo Launcher
**Effect:** 
- Torpedo salvo: re-roll d20 on attack
- Salvo Damage +1

## Mobility Talents

### Annular Confinement Jacketing
**Requirement:** 23rd century or later  
**Effect:** May attack with energy weapons at warp (Close range only, Difficulty +1)

### Improved Impulse Drive
**Effect:** When using Impulse minor action, spend 2 Momentum to increase attack Difficulty against ship by +1 until start of next turn

### Improved Reaction Control System
**Effect:** Moving through difficult terrain: Momentum cost -1 (minimum 0)

### Improved Warp Drive
**Effect:** When taking Warp major action, roll 1d20. If ≤ Engines, don't spend Reserve Power

### Variable Geometry Warp Field
**Effect:** When attempting to go to warp, select one trait affecting task and ignore it

## Sensor & Science Talents

### Advanced Research Facilities
**Requirement:** Science 3+  
**Effect:** When research task succeeds (assisted by Computers + Science), ask 2 additional questions as if Obtaining Information

### Advanced Sensor Suites
**Effect:** Tasks assisted by Sensors: ship rolls 2d20 for assistance. Cannot use with Sensors breach

### High-Resolution Sensors
**Effect:** Outside combat, successful tasks assisted by Sensors gain 1 bonus Momentum (cannot be saved)

### Improved Probe Bay
**Effect:** 
- Launch Probe: up to 5 zones away
- Probes have 3 Shield points, destroyed at 1 breach (instead of any damage)

### Modular Laboratories
**Requirement:** Science 2+  
**Effect:** Science lab opportunity cost: 0 for first lab, 1 for second

### Sophisticated Astrometrics Facilities
**Effect:** 
- Course plotting/mapping tasks: re-roll d20
- Trait created: Potency +1 automatically

## Communications Talents

### Command Ship
**Requirement:** Command 3+  
**Effect:** Command task creating trait: assisted by Communications + Command, may confer trait to allied ships/parties with comm link

### Dedicated Subspace Transceiver Array
**Effect:** Subspace/standard comm tasks: re-roll ship's assistance dice (if Communications not disabled)

### Electronic Warfare Systems
**Effect:** Intercepting/jamming enemy comms traits: spend 1 Momentum to increase Potency by 1 OR affect second enemy vessel

### Wormhole Relay System
**Requirement:** 2371 or later  
**Effect:** Science tasks to send/receive data through wormhole: Difficulty -2 (minimum 0)

## Medical Talents

### Advanced Medical Ward/Sickbay
**Requirement:** Medicine 3+  
**Effect:** 
- Gains Advanced Medical Ward/Sickbay trait (stacks with normal sickbay benefits)
- Lost if Computers disabled
- +1 Crew Support (Medical department only)

### Emergency Medical Hologram (EMH)
**Requirement:** 2371-2380  
**Effect:** 
- One additional supporting character (EMH)
- No Crew Support cost
- Only in locations with holo-emitters
- Mark II (2374): +1 Presence
- Mark III (2377): +1 Medicine  
- Mark IV (2380): Technical Expertise talent
- Obsolete after 2380 (replaced by Advanced Emergency Crew Holograms)

### Expanded Emergency Medical Facilities
**Effect:** 
- Diagnosis tasks: diagnose patients = Medical rating
- With Triage talent: +2 patients per Momentum (Repeatable)
- Extended treatment tasks: Impact +1

### Extensive Medical Laboratories
**Requirement:** Medicine 4+  
**Effect:** Extended tasks in sickbay (unknown conditions/treatments): Impact +1

## Crew & Personnel Talents

### Advanced Emergency Crew Holograms
**Requirement:** 2380 onward  
**Effect:** 
- Holographic supporting characters = half Computers score (rounded up)
- Activate/deactivate: minor action
- No Crew Support cost
- Cannot operate outside ship
- All receive same character advances

### Dedicated Personnel (X)
**Effect:** Choose one department. Gain 2 Crew Support (that department only). Repeatable

### Extensive Automation
**Requirement:** Computers 10+  
**Effect:** 
- Crew Support reduced to half (rounded up)
- Ship may attempt tasks/take turns by itself
- Add 1 Threat before task attempted

## Diplomatic & Comfort Talents

### Deluxe Galley
**Requirement:** Command 3+  
**Effect:** Once per adventure, create Fine Dining trait for free when hosting VIP. Character using Presence/Command with this trait: +1 bonus Momentum (cannot be saved)

### Diplomatic Suites
**Effect:** When hosting negotiations, crew assisted by Computers + Command OR Structure + Command

### Dual Environment
**Requirement:** 24th century or later  
**Effect:** Characters in redundant system rooms may assist others in connected rooms as if in same room

## Repair & Maintenance Talents

### Improved Damage Control
**Effect:** 
- Damage Control action: re-roll single d20
- Extended repair tasks: Impact +1

### Improved Power Systems
**Effect:** 
- Regain Power action: Difficulty -1 (minimum 1)
- Spend 1 Momentum (Immediate) to ignore complications

### Rugged Design
**Effect:** 
- Patch/repair breach tasks: re-roll d20
- If successful, spend Momentum to patch second breach (2 Momentum +1 per Potency step)

### Secondary Reactors
**Effect:** Once per scene, when taking Reroute Power action, spend 2 Momentum (Immediate) to immediately regain Reserve Power

## Special Systems Talents

### Automatic Return
**Requirement:** 23rd century or later  
**Effect:** If Computers system receives no commands for 10 days, autopilot ship to home base at maximum impulse

### Captain's Yacht
**Effect:** One additional small craft (doesn't count against capacity, doesn't use shuttlebay)

### Cloaking Device
**Requirement:** Klingon/Romulan ship or GM discretion  
**Activation:** Control + Engineering (Difficulty 2), assisted by Engines + Security. Requires Reserve Power  
**Effect:** 
- Vessel gains Cloaked trait
- Cannot attack or be attacked (unless detected)
- Shields down while cloaked
- Deactivate: minor action

### Extensive Shuttlebays
**Effect:** 
- Small Craft Readiness increased by (Scale - 1)
- May carry up to two Scale 2 small craft

### Transport Inhibitors
**Effect:** When active, all transports to/from ship fail. Ship's own transporters shut down

### Advanced Transporters
**Requirement:** 24th century or later, Science 4+  
**Effect:** With Reserve Power to Sensors, select one trait affecting transporter. Either ignore Difficulty increase OR change "impossible" to Difficulty +1

## Department Enhancement Talents

### Expansive Department (X)
**Requirement:** Department score 5  
**Effect:** Select one department (score 5). When ship assists using that department, spend 1 Momentum (Immediate, Repeatable) to ignore complication

## Silent Running Rules

**Conditions:**
- Systems at minimum
- No shields or weapons
- Movement: maneuvering thrusters only
- Passive sensors: Difficulty +1 for Sensors tasks
- Cannot be detected unless Close range or zone actively scanned

**Breaks Silent Running:**
- Moving at Impulse or Warp
- Raising shields
- Arming weapons
- Taking Reroute Power action
